Albuquerque Athletic Field Reservation System

AAFR was established in 1972, Visiting AAFR allows you to experience the authentic Southwest. As one of the oldest cities in the United States, AAFR boasts a unique multicultural history and heritage. All forms of sports are encouraged here with all facilities. You will always know you're someplace special, whether you're playing at one of our soccer field, enjoying our world-class visual and performing arts, or playing on the best golf courses in the AAFR. Nowhere is the confluence of past and present more dramatic than here in Albuquerque, where the modern city skyline is set against a backdrop of the stunning Sandia Mountains and an endless, timeless blue sky.

  • We offer all facilities for Basketball court, Soccer and Golf courts.
  • Training sessions will be provided for every team enrolled with us.
  • Pavilion Facilities are provided here.
